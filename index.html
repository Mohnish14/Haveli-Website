About Shri Vitthalesh Foundation

Mission: To provide Pushtimargya books, literature, and resources to devotees worldwide, preserving and spreading the teachings of Shrinathji and Pushtimarg.

Vision: To unite devotees worldwide, fostering devotion and spiritual growth through accessible resources and shared knowledge.

What We Offer:

  • Rare and authentic Pushtimargya books and scriptures.
  • Educational workshops and events on Pushtimarg principles.
  • Support for preservation of temples and havelis.
  • Weekly Paathshaala sessions for spiritual learning.

Join us in preserving the spiritual heritage of Pushtimarg by participating in our initiatives or exploring our resources!
